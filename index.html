About Universe Builders Inc.

Simulation is a powerful tool for teaching a better understanding our world, and allowing people to push boundaries while taking no risk. All of science is a tool to model our world, and a simulation is simply a way to enact those models.

We dream of creating authoring tools that permit complex simulations to exist, by allowing distributed computing to divide a simulated world into separate unique parts that all talk to each other.

We also understand that very simple simulations, represented through games, allow for extremely fun, even addicting, forms of education. This is where we are getting started before tackling the larger challenges.
